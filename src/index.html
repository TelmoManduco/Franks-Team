Our Mission

Manchester's Home for Fitness and Community

At Frank's Team, we're more than just a gym. We are a registered Community Amateur Sports Club (CASC) dedicated to making fitness and martial arts accessible to everyone. We welcome all ages, from kids as young as 6 to adults, helping you get fit, learn new skills, and thrive together.

Located right here in the heart of Manchester, we actively invest in our local area, creating programs and opportunities that go beyond the mat. Join our supportive family and discover the power of community-driven fitness.
