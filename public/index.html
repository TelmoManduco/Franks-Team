Our Mission

Manchester's Home for Fitness and Community

At Frank's Team, we're more than just a gym. We are a registered Community Amateur Sports Club (CASC) dedicated to making fitness and martial arts accessible to everyone. We welcome all ages, from kids as young as 6 to adults, helping you get fit, learn new skills, and thrive together.

Located right here in the heart of Manchester, we actively invest in our local area, creating programs and opportunities that go beyond the mat. Join our supportive family and discover the power of community-driven fitness.

OUR DIFFERENCE

Why Choose Franks Team?

We offer specialised training, a commitment to community, and a family-friendly environment you won't find anywhere else.

Registered Community Club

As a registered CASC, we invest directly back into our local community, ensuring fitness is accessible and supportive for all Manchester residents.

Specialised Martial Arts

Expert coaching in various martial arts, including boxing and kickboxing, to build discipline, strength, and confidence for every member.

Family-Friendly Training

Tailored programs for everyone: adults seeking fitness, competitive athletes, and children starting their martial arts journey from age 6.
